Hi there! I’m Han. I am currently finishing up my last year studying City Planning/Transportation at University of Pennsylvania.

I am originally from Beijing, and graduated from Wellesley College, MA, with a double major in Mathematics and Media Arts and Sciences. Two big parts of my passion are math/data analysis and art/design. I enjoy the mathematical and logical reasonings behind data analysis, and at the same time, I enjoy the freedom of expressing myself and connecting with other people through design. I am studying city planning where I can combine my two interests to create products that bring positive impact to the world. I hope to work in the autonomous vehicle industry to better understand people’s traveling needs and create better designs for a more enjoyable, sustainable and equitable future mobility.

I play the violin and the viola. I doodle. I ice skate. I enjoy thinking through random questions. I’m always excited to meet people for music jamming and sharing our brilliant thoughts together!
