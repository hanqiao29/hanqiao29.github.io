Design + Data

I. Design Projects

Delaware Avenue South Plan

This is a semester long project, where we worked in a team of 7 to identify issues and opportunities along Delaware Avenue and in adjacent neighborhoods. The final product is a book which contains graphics, maps, and analysis to deliver recommendations for the future development in the study area.

Birds of Wellesley

This is a zine about eight different birds on Wellesley Campus. The zine is created after two months of bird watching for two hours every day.

II. Data Analysis Projects

Travel Mode Choice

Analyzed the 2012 DVRPC Household Travel Survey using data visualization and logistic regression. Built machine learning models using SciKitLearn and MxNet to predict travel model choice with predictors of socio-economic characteristics of travelers.

Who Takes the Bus

This is a GIS project investigating the relationship between SEPTA bus ridership and income level of residents in the surrounding area.
